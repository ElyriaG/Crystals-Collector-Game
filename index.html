Crystal Game

Game Instructions

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, but be careful! If you go over you lose.

  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you. The only way to figure it out is by clicking the crystal.

  • At the start of each game, the value of each crystal changes.
